Selectors must be lower case letters and forward slashes. Selectors may be qualified with an origin or wildcard '@*'. Use '*' wildcard for all local tags. Must start with a letter and must not contain two non-letters slashes in a row. Private tags start with an underscore.
(i.e. "*", "science", "science@origin" "my/tag@*", or "_my/private/tag")
Selector must not be blank.

Queries support AND (:), OR (|) and grouping qualified tags (parentheses). Tags must be lower case letters and forward slashes. Tags may be qualified with an origin. Must not start with a slash or contain two forward slashes in a row. Private tags start with an underscore.
(i.e. "science:news", "science@origin science@other" "your/tag my/tag", or "news:_my/private/tag")
Query must not be blank.
